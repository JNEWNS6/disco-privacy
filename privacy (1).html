Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-10-17

Disco (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

  • Local Data: The extension stores settings, preferences, and cached promo code data in your browser’s local storage.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or transmit personal identifying information.
  • No Browsing History: Disco only runs on checkout pages of e‑commerce sites to offer promo codes. It does not track activity on unrelated pages.

2. How We Use Information

  • To remember your extension settings and onboarding state.
  • To suggest or apply available promo codes on supported sites.
  • To maintain performance and reliability.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. If future features use a secure backend API, it will handle only anonymous coupon lookup requests over HTTPS.

4. Security

All processing occurs locally in your browser. Any network requests (if present) are sent over encrypted HTTPS connections.

5. Your Choices

  • You can disable or remove the extension from your browser’s Extensions page at any time.
  • You can clear the extension’s local data via your browser settings.

6. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at support@discoextension.com.
